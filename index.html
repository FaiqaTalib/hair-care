Hair Loss (Alopecia)

The most common hair problem affecting millions worldwide. Understanding the root cause is the first step to recovery.

Causes

  • Genetic/hereditary factors
  • Hormonal imbalance
  • Poor nutrition & vitamin deficiency
  • Chronic stress
  • Scalp infections
  • Over-styling with heat

Treatments

  • Minoxidil (topical)
  • PRP therapy
  • Biotin supplements
  • Laser therapy (LLLT)
  • Scalp massage routine
  • Hair transplant surgery

Home Remedies

  • Onion juice application
  • Castor oil massage
  • Egg yolk hair mask
  • Green tea rinse
  • Aloe vera scalp gel

Prevention

  • Balanced, protein-rich diet
  • Gentle hair handling
  • Reduce heat styling
  • Manage stress levels
  • Regular scalp oiling

Premature Graying

Graying before age 25 (in Asians) is considered premature. It's driven by melanin loss in hair follicles.

Causes

  • Genetic predisposition
  • Vitamin B12 deficiency
  • Thyroid disorders
  • Oxidative stress
  • Smoking habit
  • Autoimmune conditions

Solutions

  • B12 & folic acid supplements
  • Copper-rich diet
  • Quit smoking
  • Thyroid management
  • Antioxidant-rich foods

Home Remedies

  • Amla (Indian gooseberry) oil
  • Black sesame paste
  • Curry leaves in coconut oil
  • Henna + coffee paste
  • Rosemary oil treatment

Products

  • Melanin-boosting serums
  • Color-safe shampoos
  • Deep conditioning masks
  • Biotin supplements
  • UV-protection sprays
🌱

Hair Growth Issues

Slow or stunted hair growth affects confidence. The right nutrition, scalp care, and treatments can make a dramatic difference.

Causes

  • Nutritional deficiencies
  • Poor scalp circulation
  • Hormonal disruptions
  • Over-processing & chemicals
  • Anemia (low iron)
  • Crash dieting

Treatments

  • Scalp massages daily
  • Dermarolling (0.5mm)
  • Biotin injections
  • Iron & zinc supplements
  • Protein-rich diet

Home Remedies

  • Aloe vera gel on scalp
  • Rosemary oil massage
  • Rice water rinse
  • Fenugreek seed paste
  • Peppermint oil serum

Foods for Growth

  • Eggs (complete protein)
  • Spinach & leafy greens
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Nuts & seeds
  • Fatty fish (omega-3)
🛡️

Premature Baldness

Androgenetic alopecia (pattern baldness) is the most common form. Early intervention significantly improves outcomes.

Causes

  • DHT hormone sensitivity
  • Genetic inheritance
  • Autoimmune disorders
  • Traction (tight styles)
  • Medications side effects
  • Severe nutritional lack

Medical Options

  • Finasteride (oral)
  • Minoxidil (topical)
  • Hair transplant (FUE)
  • PRP injections
  • Low-level laser therapy

Natural Approach

  • Saw palmetto supplement
  • Pumpkin seed oil
  • Scalp massage routine
  • Reduce DHT via diet
  • Anti-inflammatory diet

Prevention

  • Early diagnosis & treatment
  • Avoid tight hairstyles
  • Reduce chemical use
  • Manage DHT levels
  • Regular dermatologist visits
