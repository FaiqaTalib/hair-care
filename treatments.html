Hair Loss

Onion Juice Application

Sulphur-rich onion stimulates dormant hair follicles and improves scalp blood flow.

Ingredients: Fresh onion, cotton ball, strainer

1

Grate or blend 1 onion and strain out the juice.

2

Apply to scalp with a cotton ball, massage gently.

3

Leave 30 mins, then rinse with mild shampoo.

Stimulates follicles, reduces thinning
🍋

Dandruff

Lemon & Coconut Oil

Antifungal lemon combined with nourishing coconut oil tackles dandruff at the root.

Ingredients: 2 tbsp lemon juice, 3 tbsp coconut oil

1

Warm coconut oil and mix with fresh lemon juice.

2

Massage gently into scalp for 10 minutes.

3

Leave 20 mins, then wash off completely.

Reduces dandruff, soothes scalp
🌵

Hair Growth

Aloe Vera Gel

Enzymes in aloe vera repair dead skin cells on the scalp and promote faster hair growth.

Ingredients: Fresh aloe vera leaf

1

Extract fresh gel from a cut aloe vera leaf.

2

Apply evenly along scalp and hair length.

3

Leave 1 hour or overnight, then rinse.

Promotes cell growth, reduces inflammation
🥚

Dry Hair

Egg & Yogurt Mask

Protein-packed egg with probiotic yogurt deeply conditions and restores shine to dry hair.

Ingredients: 1 egg, 3 tbsp plain yogurt

1

Beat egg and mix thoroughly with yogurt.

2

Apply from roots to tips, cover all hair.

3

Shower cap 30 mins, rinse with cool water.

Deep conditioning, adds natural shine
